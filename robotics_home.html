    Our Team

    Poly Prep's robotics teams are made up of a diverse group of students. We have representation from over 5 ethnicities and the majority of our team is made up of people of color. Although our team was created in 2019-2020, we've had to postpone our meetings and competitions because of COVID-19. Since then, we have recreated our team, the Blazing Bluebots, and started a second team, Polymorphism, a year later. We strive to improve our skills, uplift others, and encourage people in minoritized groups, such as people who are Black, Latinx, Asian, and/or female, to join and learn about robotics.
